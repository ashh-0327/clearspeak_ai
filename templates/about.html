About ClearSpeak AI

ClearSpeak AI is an intelligent legal document simplifier powered by cutting-edge Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology.

Our mission is to make legal language accessible to everyone — whether you're a student trying to understand a lease agreement, a professional reviewing business contracts, or simply someone who wants to know what they're signing.

What We Do

We analyze complex legal documents and transform them into clear, plain-English explanations. Our AI identifies key terms, flags potential risks, and provides actionable insights — all in seconds.

How It Works

Upload or paste any legal document, and our AI engine — powered by Llama 3.3 via Groq — will analyze the content, cross-reference it against a curated legal knowledge base using RAG, and deliver a structured breakdown including summaries, risk assessments, and comprehension quizzes.

Our Technology

NLP & LLM: We use advanced large language models for deep document understanding.

RAG: Retrieval-augmented generation ensures our explanations are grounded in real legal context, not hallucinated content.

Domain Detection: Our classifier automatically identifies the type of legal document to provide domain-specific analysis.
